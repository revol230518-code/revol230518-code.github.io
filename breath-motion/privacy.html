Privacy Policy — Breath & Motion

Last updated: 2026-02-08

本ページは英語を主とし、日本語補助を併記しています。

1. Overview

Breath & Motion is a wellness timer app. We aim to minimize data collection. This app displays ads.

本アプリは呼吸・運動のタイマーです。収集情報は最小化しますが、広告を表示します。

2. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to provide personal information such as name or email within the app.

氏名・メール等の入力を求める設計ではありません。

Advertising data: When ads are shown, our advertising partners may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Advertising/Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address (general location inference)
  • Device information (OS, language, app version)
  • Ad interactions and diagnostics (impressions, clicks, crash/performance metrics)

広告表示に伴い、広告ID、IPアドレス、端末情報、広告の反応/診断情報等が広告事業者側で収集される場合があります。

3. Local Storage

Timer settings and preferences may be stored locally on your device to provide app functionality.

4. Advertising (Third-Party Services)

This app may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads).

These providers may process data to deliver and measure ads, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region and device settings.

本アプリは第三者広告（例：Google AdMob）を利用する場合があります。地域や端末設定により、パーソナライズ広告/非パーソナライズ広告が配信されることがあります。

5. Your Choices / Opt-Out

  • You can reset or limit the use of your Advertising ID in your device settings.
  • You can manage ad personalization settings in your Google settings (if applicable).

端末設定から広告IDのリセットや広告のパーソナライズ設定を変更できます。

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age required by applicable laws for personalized advertising. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us.

本アプリは、法律上パーソナライズ広告に同意できない年齢の子どもを対象にしていません。

7. International Users

If you are located in certain regions (e.g., EEA/UK/Switzerland), you may be shown non-personalized ads or asked for consent depending on the advertising provider’s requirements.

EEA/UK/スイス等では、同意の扱いにより非パーソナライズ広告になる場合があります。

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
